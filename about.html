About Me

Get to know me better

Hi! I'm Caterina Finelli, a Senior Product Designer with a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences. I specialize in translating complex challenges into intuitive, elegant solutions that resonate with users.

My journey spans fintech, mobility, and digital health — industries where thoughtful design can genuinely improve lives. I believe design is not decoration, but clarity. It's about understanding the problem deeply and crafting solutions that feel inevitable.

Beyond design, I'm the host of Che Viaggio, a podcast exploring culture, design, and the stories that shape our world. I'm driven by curiosity and the belief that great design emerges from great conversations.
